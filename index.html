Fundamentals

What is the Objective?

Get to the enemy side of the map once
In Rainmaker, you only need to reach the enemy base a single time to win. Because of this, the mode revolves around finding good positions along a path to the enemy base.

Unlike Splat Zones and Clam Blitz, you don’t need to spawn camp for any extended period of time; instead, the focus is on taking guaranteed space as quickly as possible.

Take space quickly
In this mode, the objective moves as fast as you do, so speed is absolutely essential to win. Pushes and counter pushes are most effective when they happen immediately, so be sure to bring a kit that can take space in the shortest amount of time possible.

Be ready to intecept other players at all times
When you push into the enemy base, you’ll want to intercept the enemy team as they come to defend so that they can't affect the Rainmaker.

On the flip side, when the enemy team is pushing into your base, you’ll want to intercept the Rainmaker so that it can’t continue forward.

Since the Rainmaker carrier can rotate quickly, you’ll need to be able to rotate just as quickly between defensive positions. Swim speed is essential here.
